# Callsign Media Landing Page Design

## Brand
- **Company:** Callsign Media
- **Tone:** Clean & Premium
- **Theme:** Light/white with subtle accents
- **Primary Goal:** Drive consulting leads (Book a Consultation)
- **Secondary Goal:** Showcase product portfolio as credibility

## Page Structure

### 1. Hero
- White background with subtle geometric accent
- Company name in bold, refined typography
- Tagline: "We build AI-powered products and help businesses do the same."
- CTAs: "Book a Consultation" (primary) + "See Our Work" (secondary outlined)

### 2. Consulting Services (light gray bg)
Five service cards with icons:
1. **AI Strategy & Roadmap** - Audit workflows, identify AI opportunities, phased adoption plan
2. **AI Product Development** - Custom AI features from prototype to production
3. **Developer Workflow Automation** - AI dev tooling for faster shipping
4. **AI Integration & Migration** - Add AI to existing products (RAG, search, generation)
5. **AI Training & Workshops** - Hands-on team training with AI tools

### 3. Our Products (white bg)
Three product cards with logo, screenshot, description, link:
- Lowkey Media Viewer - desktop image/video viewer
- ModelPad - AI writing & business platform
- Samson - iOS fitness tracking app

### 4. Why Callsign Media (light gray bg)
Three differentiators:
- "We Ship Products" - not just consultants, we build and maintain production software
- "Full Stack AI Expertise" - from LLM integrations to computer vision to mobile
- "Pragmatic, Not Hype" - focused on real ROI, not buzzwords

### 5. CTA Banner (dark bg)
"Ready to bring AI into your business?" with Book a Consultation button

### 6. Footer
Copyright, links, contact email

## Technical Approach
- Single static HTML file with inline CSS
- System font stack (zero external dependencies)
- Fully responsive, mobile-first
- Product logos/screenshots copied from sibling repos
- Placeholder images where needed

## Assets to Use
- Lowkey: logo.png, screenshot.jpg from lowkey-homepage
- ModelPad: modelpad.svg from modelpad/public/assets
- Samson: samson.png, icon.png from samson/assets/images
