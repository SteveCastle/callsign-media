# Callsign Media Landing Page — Messaging Restructure

**Date:** 2026-03-01
**Status:** Approved

## Goal

Reposition Callsign Media from a pure software studio / AI consultancy to a comprehensive AI-powered growth and technology partner — covering software, marketing, sales funnels, SEM, experimentation, and process automation.

## Approach

Pillars model: 6 unified service cards blending tech + marketing under one AI/experimentation theme. Products section stays as-is.

## Hero

- **Headline:** "AI-powered software, marketing, and growth — engineered to convert."
- **Subtext:** "Callsign Media combines software engineering, sales funnel design, SEM expertise, and AI automation into one data-driven growth partner. We build, measure, and optimize everything."
- **CTAs:** "Book a Consultation" + "See Our Work" (unchanged)

## Services — "What We Do"

- **Heading:** "Full-stack growth, from code to conversion"
- **Subtext:** "We don't separate 'tech' from 'marketing.' Every service is built on experimentation, automation, and AI optimization."

### Cards (6):

1. **AI-Powered Sales Funnels** — Lead capture, nurture sequences, and conversion optimization driven by AI. Funnels that learn from data and improve automatically.
2. **SEM & Paid Acquisition** — Google Ads, bid strategy, keyword optimization, AI-driven campaign management. Lower CAC while scaling spend.
3. **Experimentation & Analytics** — A/B testing, multivariate experiments, custom dashboards, metrics pipelines. Every decision backed by data.
4. **Process & Workflow Automation** — CRM automation, marketing ops, internal tooling, AI-powered workflows that eliminate manual work.
5. **Custom Software & AI Products** — Web, desktop, mobile — production software with AI baked in. LLM integrations, computer vision, intelligent search.
6. **AI Strategy & Team Training** — Workshops, adoption roadmaps, ongoing advisory across marketing, sales, and engineering.

## Why Us — "Engineers who think like marketers"

- **Subtext:** "Most agencies do marketing or engineering. We do both — and connect them through experimentation and AI."

1. **We Ship Real Software** — Production apps across desktop, web, mobile. Advice from building, not theorizing.
2. **Experimentation Is Core** — A/B testing, metrics, data-driven iteration from day one.
3. **AI Across the Stack** — From ad bidding to code generation to funnel optimization. AI where it moves numbers.

## CTA Banner

- **Heading:** "Ready to turn your growth engine into a machine?"
- **Subtext:** "Let's talk about where AI, automation, and experimentation can drive real results across your business."

## Products Section

No changes. Stays as-is (Lowkey, ModelPad, Samson).

## Constraints

- Same page layout/structure and visual design
- Content-only changes (hero, services, why us, CTA)
- Services grid goes from 3+2 to 3+3 (two rows of 3)
